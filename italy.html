4) Notes & Tips

  • Universitaly is the official pre-enrolment/visa portal for non-EU visa-seekers. Create your account and prep uploads early.
  • Regional scholarships (ER.GO, EDISU, DiSCo, DSU) apply to both Bachelor’s & Master’s and can fully cover fees + housing/meals if you meet income/merit rules.
  • Always verify the latest deadlines & calls on official pages before submitting.
  • Have certified translations ready (English/Italian as requested).
  • Apply early to avoid last-minute portal slowdowns or document issues.
  • Consider multiple scholarship routes simultaneously for backup options.
  • Budget for visa, travel, and initial settlement costs even if tuition is waived.
