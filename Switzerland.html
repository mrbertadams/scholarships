4) Notes & Tips

  • Translations must be certified into the language of instruction (English, French, German, or Italian).
  • Use https://www.swissuniversities.ch to explore and compare all Swiss universities.
  • Apply to multiple scholarships in parallel; competition is very high.
  • Prepare proof of finances even if applying for scholarships; Swiss embassies require minimum ~CHF 21,000/year.
