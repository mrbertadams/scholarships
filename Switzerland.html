Switzerland Scholarship & University Guide (2026 Edition) for Students from Ghana

1. Standard Documents Checklist

Required documents for Bachelor's, Master's, and PhD applications in Switzerland
Document Name Notes
Academic Transcripts Must be officially translated into English, French, German, or Italian (depending on the university).
Diploma/Certificate Notarized copies of high school (Bachelor's), Bachelor's (Master's), or Master's (PhD).
Passport Copy Must be valid for at least 6 months beyond the intended stay.
CV/Resume For Master's and PhD, must include academic and professional experience.
Motivation Letter Tailored to each university/scholarship (1-2 pages).
Recommendation Letters 2-3 letters (academic for Bachelor's/Master's, academic + professional for PhD).
Language Proficiency TOEFL (80+), IELTS (6.0+), or equivalent for English; DELF/DALF for French; Goethe-Zertifikat for German.
Research Proposal (PhD) Required for PhD applications (3-5 pages).
Proof of Funds Bank statements (min. CHF 21,000/year for student visa).

4. Notes & Tips Section

✅ Helpful Actions:
  • Apply to multiple scholarships to increase chances.
  • Start document preparation early (translations take time).
⚠️ Reminders:
  • Check university-specific language requirements.
  • PhD applicants must contact supervisors before applying.
❌ Warnings:
  • Do not miss deadlines—Swiss universities are strict.
  • Proof of funds is mandatory for visa (CHF 21,000/year).
